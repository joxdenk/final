About Bugema University

Bugema University was established in 1945. It is one of best institution in Uganda offering different courses. Bugema University is located 34km away from Kampala, the capital of the country. This make it a better place for learnig.

Bugema University is an excellent and distinctive wholistic christian institution designed to prepare students through training, research and scholarship for productive lives of useful service to God and to the Society with comitment, integrity, honesty and loyalty.

Bugema has helped a lot of people to achieve their dreams in different field of professions the has made made it one of the best institution of learnig in Uganda and east Africa.
